# Confusion Of Objectives

**Also Known As:**

n/a


## Summary

Confusion of Objectives is an anti-pattern in which a manager is unable to focus consistently on the shared objectives of the team or project. Attention is diverted to personal goals, political concerns, career-related interests, or external pressures. As a result, critical managerial decisions are delayed or avoided, leaving the team without direction and causing essential tasks to stall while team members remain unaware of the underlying issues.

-	Progress slows due to lack of guidance and alignment.
